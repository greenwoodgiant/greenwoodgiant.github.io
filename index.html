Who is this guy, anyway?

I was born in Baton Rouge, Louisiana to a couple of right-brained engineers, but forged my own left-brained path as a theatre major at LSU. In Los Angeles, I worked steadily performing classics on stage and in the background on film/tv for nearly eight years.

After growing tired of the starving part of the starving artist lifestyle, I moved to Austin where I was introduced to the world of software development by way of working as a recruiter for Python and Go developers. I was also introduced to the world of uncle-hood by my new niece, Eleanor.

I found myself constantly more interested in the jobs I was hunting than the job I was doing, and was baffled how "attention to detail" could be a negative trait, so I decided to leave Sales behind and joined the Data Analysis and Visualization Boot Camp through UT to learn all the excellent technologies I'd been scouring resumes for previously.

I've always considered myself a storyteller, and I'm excited to see what kind of stories I can unveil through the use of Data Analysis!
